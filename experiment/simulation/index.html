Ultrasonic Sensor Interfacing with Raspberry Pi and Temperature/Humidity Reading Program

    General Instruction

  • Click on the components to start interacting it with in the connection area
  • Hover over the raspberry Pi connections to get informations about various connections pin
  • Double click on various connections points to make connections
  • Double click on the empty space in the connection area to extend and bend wire connections

How To Connect

  1. Make a voltage divider circuit using two resistors and connecting one end of the pin to another
  2. Connect the Vcc pin to 5V PWR
  3. Connect the trig pin to GPIO 18
  4. Connect the Gnd pin to Ground
  5. Connect 1k pin which is connected with GPIO 24 to Ground
  6. Connect 2k pin which is connected with 1k to Ground

After completing these steps, you can change the temperature of the surroundings via the blue bottom bar and observe the result in the display.
