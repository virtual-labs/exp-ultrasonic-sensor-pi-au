Ultrasonic Sensor Interfacing with Raspberry Pi and Temperature/Humidity Reading Program

Aim : To interface LED with Raspberry Pi and write a program to turn ON LED or 1 sec after every 2 seconds.

Instructions

  • Click on the components to start interacting it with in the connection area
  • Hover over the rasberrypi connections to get informations about various connections pin
  • Double click on various connections points to make connections
  • Double click on the empty space in the connection area to extend and bend wire connections

Procedure

These are the steps to connect an ultrasonic sensor to a Raspberry Pi pin:

  1. Connect the VCC pin of the sensor to the 3.3V power of the Raspberry Pi.
  2. Make a voltage divider circuit using two resistors and connecting one end of the pin to another
  3. Connect the trig pin to GPIO 11
  4. Connect the echo pin to the 1k resistor
  5. Connect 2k pin which is connected with 1k to GPIO 12

After completing these steps, you can change the temperature of the surroundings via the blue bottom bar and observe the result in the display.
