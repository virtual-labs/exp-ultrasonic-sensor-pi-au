General Instruction

  • Click on the components to start interacting it with in the connection area
  • Hover over the raspberry Pi connections to get informations about various connections pin
  • Double click on various connections points to make connections
  • Double click on the empty space in the connection area to extend and bend wire connections

How To Connect

  1. Make a voltage divider circuit using two resistors and connecting one end of the pin to another
  2. Connect the Vcc of Ultrasonic Sensor to pin to 5V PWR of Raspberry Pi
  3. Connect the trig pin of Ultrasonic Sensor to GPIO 18 of Raspberry Pi
  4. Connect the Gnd of Ultrasonic Sensor to Ground of Raspberry Pi
  5. Connect the Echo pin of Ultrasonic Sensor to 1K Resistor
  6. Connect 1K Resistor pin which is connected with GPIO 24 to Ground
  7. Connect 2K Resistor pin which is connected with 1K Resistor to Ground

After completing these steps, you can change the positon of object and observe the result in the display.
